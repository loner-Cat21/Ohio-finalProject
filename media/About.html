Ohio, The Heart of it All

Ohio is a state in the United States of America located on the northeastern edge of the Midwest region. Lake Erie borders the north, Pennsylvania borders the east, West Virginia and Kentucky border the southeast and south, Indiana borders the west, and Michigan borders the northwest.

Temperatures in Ohio are similar to those in the north-central and eastern United States, with summer highs seldom exceeding 100 degrees Fahrenheit and winter lows rarely falling below 20 degrees Fahrenheit. On a typical July day, the temperature will range from the mid-60s F to the mid-80s F, although it will reach a high in the mid-30s F in January.
